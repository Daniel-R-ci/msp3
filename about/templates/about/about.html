About us

The ShutterClickers Photoclub was founded in 2021 by students at Springfieldville Collage. While many of our members are students, we welcome any and all interested in the various aspects of photography.

We have two meetings every month, one where we focus on taking pictures and one more theoretical when we look at pictures, learn about camera settings, editing and so on. The schedule is sent out four times a year to all members.
